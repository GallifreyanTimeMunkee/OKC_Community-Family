SKY RINK

July 01, 2020 to September 07, 2020

Come visit the new outdoor roller skating rink! The Sky Rink at Scissortail Park is located in the southeast corner of the Park near the Skydance Bridge. The rink sits under the Event Pavilion and has approximately 5,000 square feet of skating surface for both traditional quad skates as well as inline skates. Skates are available to rent, but personal skates are always welcome.

Canadian County Free Fair

August 26, 2020 to August 28, 2020

Free Fair fun!

The Canadian County Free Fair, an annual tradition in El Reno since 1914, usually features a carnival, live entertainment on multiple stages, live stock exhibits, arts & craft exhibits, baking competitions, antique tractor pull and more. But most of the activities have been canceled for 2020. There will be a show-&-go for junior livestock entries. Watch the competition live of Facebook.

Apache Balloon Fest

August 28, 2020 to August 30, 2020

Dont miss! The annual Apache Balloon Fest

Features tethered balloon rides, night glows, a carnival midway, food trucks, live music and more. Visit: https://www.apacheballoonfest.com for more information.
